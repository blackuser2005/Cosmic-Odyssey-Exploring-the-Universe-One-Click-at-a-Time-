Haumea: The Elliptical Wonder

Haumea, a dwarf planet beyond Neptune, is known for its unique elongated shape, caused by its rapid rotation. It has two moons, Hiʻiaka and Namaka, named after Hawaiian goddesses.

Haumea’s icy surface and distinctive ring system make it one of the most intriguing objects in the Kuiper Belt. Its rapid rotation period is just 3.9 hours, the fastest of any known large object in the solar system.
